Do I need editing experience?

Not at all! Athleo is built to be simple and intuitive. If you can upload a video, you can build highlights. It's perfect for busy parents, youth athletes, and coaches alike.

Can I use Athleo.ai for other sports?

Yes! Athleo works best with footage where:

  • Players are clearly visible throughout most of the action
  • The video captures full-body movements (works especially well with court and field sports)
  • There's a reasonable number of players in frame (ideally fewer than 20)

We're continuously improving our technology to support more sports and filming styles.
