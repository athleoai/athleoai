FRONTEND ENGINEER

Location: Remote

Company Description

Athleo.ai is redefining how the sports community captures and showcases talent through AI-powered video storytelling. With a mission to connect and inspire the global sports community, we aim to build the world’s leading platform for capturing, amplifying, and sharing sports stories. As an early-stage startup, we’re seeking a talented Frontend Engineer to drive the next phase of our product evolution and shape the future of sports video content.

Job Responsibilities

  • Develop and enhance Athleo Creator – our cutting-edge web-based sports video editing tool.
  • Implement intuitive, high-performance UI components using React, TypeScript, and Vite.
  • Optimize real-time video rendering and processing workflows.
  • Work with streaming media processing and video engines to enable smooth playback, editing, and effects.
  • Integrate cloud-based solutions (Azure or AWS) for hosting, storage, and video processing.
  • Collaborate with backend engineers and designers to build an engaging, scalable, and interactive video editing platform.
  • Ensure cross-browser compatibility, performance optimization, and mobile responsiveness.

Requirements

  • Strong expertise in React, TypeScript, Vite, and state management (e.g., Redux, Zustand).
  • Deep understanding of web-based video editing and streaming media processing.
  • Hands-on experience with video engines (e.g., FFmpeg, WebCodecs).
  • Proficiency in handling media formats, codecs, and adaptive streaming protocols (HLS, DASH).
  • Solid knowledge of performance optimization and WebGL/WebGPU for video rendering.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a fast-paced, iterative development environment.

Bonus Skills

  • Experience with cloud architecture and services (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
  • Knowledge of server-side video transcoding, GPU acceleration, and edge computing.
  • Background in building social media or content-driven applications.
  • Passion for sports technology and interactive storytelling.

How to Apply

If you’re excited about creating the future of sports content and have the vision and skills to build an exceptional user interface for our platform, we’d love to hear from you. Please send your resume, portfolio, or LinkedIn profile along with a brief note on why you’re interested in this role to derrick@athleo.ai.
