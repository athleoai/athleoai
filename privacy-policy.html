Privacy Policy for Athleo.ai

Last Updated: March 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Athleo.ai (“Athleo”, “we”, “our” or “us”) collects, stores, uses, and discloses personal data when you use our software and services. This is an early version of our policy and may be updated after further legal review.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • User Information: Name, email, phone number, and business details when you register or interact with our platform.
  • Usage Data: Login credentials, device details, IP addresses, and other technical information related to platform access.
  • Customer Data: Any data submitted by our business customers through the use of our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our software services.
  • To communicate with users regarding account activity, updates, and support.
  • To analyze and enhance platform performance and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or security risks.

3. Data Storage and Security

We store and process personal data in locations necessary for service delivery. We implement reasonable security measures to protect personal data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that assist with hosting, analytics, and other operational functions.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or necessary for compliance with regulations.
  • Affiliated Companies: If required for internal business purposes.

5. Your Rights

Users may have rights to:

  • Access, update, or delete their personal data.
  • Opt out of promotional communications.
  • Contact us regarding any privacy concerns at sportsathleo@gmail.com.

6. Underage Data Processing

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without verified parental consent, we will take steps to delete the information promptly. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us at sportsathleo@gmail.com.

7. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through our platform or website.

For any questions or concerns, please contact us at sportsathleo@gmail.com.
