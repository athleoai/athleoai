FOUNDING ENGINEER (POTENTIAL CTO)

Company Description

Athleo.ai is redefining how the sports community captures and showcases talent through AI-powered video storytelling. With a mission to connect and inspire the global sports community, we aim to build the world’s leading platform for capturing, amplifying, and sharing sports stories. As an early-stage startup, we’re seeking a visionary Founding Engineer to drive the next phase of AI-driven content innovation and shape the future of sports technology.

Founding Engineer Job Requirements

We are seeking a talented founding engineer to lead the technical vision and development of our sports-focused video social media platform. The ideal candidate is a hands-on technical leader with expertise in web service development, scalable architecture, and cutting-edge AI/ML for video analysis.

Must-Have Experience
  • Proven expertise in web service development, including cloud-based backends and scalable architectures.
  • Strong skills in stream media processing and web-based video engine development.
  • Hands-on coding experience with modern programming languages and frameworks.
  • Leadership and team-building capabilities in a fast-paced startup environment.
  • Passion for sports and a deep understanding of how technology enhances sports experiences.
Good-to-Have Experience
  • Familiarity with AI/ML for video analysis, recommendation systems, and content personalization.
  • Experience designing engaging and responsive front-end user interfaces.
  • Expertise in cloud platforms such as AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure.
  • Knowledge of video streaming technologies (e.g., HLS, DASH) and media codecs.
  • Background in building social media or content-driven applications.

Qualifications

  • Minimum of 5+ years of experience in software development with a track record of leading technical teams.
  • Demonstrated success in developing and deploying scalable, high-performance platforms.
  • Strong understanding of video technology, cloud architecture, and modern software development practices.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) and DevOps practices.
  • Deep understanding of social media architecture and user engagement metrics.
  • Exceptional problem-solving skills and the ability to make high-stakes decisions in a fast-paced startup environment.
  • Strong leadership and communication skills, with a track record of building and scaling engineering teams.
  • Passion for sports and a deep understanding of the sports tech ecosystem is a plus.

What We Offer

  • A key leadership role in shaping a disruptive sports video social media platform.
  • Equity options.
  • A collaborative, fast-paced, and passionate team working to redefine sports storytelling.
  • The ability to work at the intersection of sports, technology, and community impact.

How to Apply

If you’re excited about creating the future of sports content and have the vision and skills to lead the technical charge, we’d love to hear from you. Please send your resume, portfolio, or LinkedIn profile along with a brief note on why you’re interested in this role to derrick@athleo.ai.
